✨ Features

Everything your cat's pantry needs

From tracking what's expiring to logging every meal— Pet Pantry has you covered.

🥫

Smart Inventory Tracking

Know exactly what you have, grouped by expiration date. Color-coded alerts tell you what's expiring soon so nothing goes to waste.

🍽️

Meal Logging

Log every meal with one tap. Inventory auto-deducts when you feed your cat— or skip tracking entirely and just log meals for fun.

🐱

Multi-Pet Profiles

Have more than one cat? No problem. Create profiles for all your pets and track each one's meals and food preferences separately.

⭐

Cat Ratings

Let your cat rate their meals! Log a 1–5 star rating so you always remember which foods were a hit and which were a flop.

🐾 How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No apps to download, no subscriptions. Just open, set up, and start tracking.

1

Create your profile

Sign up in seconds. Add your cat's profile with name, breed, and age.

2

Add your food stash

3
